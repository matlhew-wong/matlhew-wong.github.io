It's almost 5, what am I doing.

Posted by Matthew on December 12, 2016

Let me describe what the setting is right now for me. It's 4:47 and I'm on the third floor of the Bahen Centre for Information Technology. This is a terrible introduction but just stay with me here. Finals season just means no class but same amount of work and for me that's just weird. I like going to class and without it, the extra time is just being dedicated to stuff like this. Which brings me back to the reason I'm up at 4:57.

For those who don't know me, go read my bio. If I havn't put my bio up yet because I've passed out then all you need to know is that I go to U of T and I'm terrible at writing, English in general actually. So if you see any grammar or spelling mistakes, shoot me an email and I'll be sure to do nothing about it.

So back to what i'm doing. If I'm not studying, I'm probably wasting time surfing the web and reading pointless stuff. But recently, everyone's working hard to glitter themselves up so that they can get 'jobs'. Turns out the stuff they teach us at U of T won't help you land a job so you have to teach it yourself or something. I know it's weird. Anyhow, that's what i'm doing right now. Collecting all the average projects I've done over the years and present them in a way that they make me look half decent. So if that's one thing you get out of this mess of writing, prepare now so you can sleep early in the future :)

So to sum up how I'm feeling right now in one sentence, 'I'm glad I didn't slip and fall from the ice outside'. You thought I was going to say something inspirational. HA Jokes on you. Try again next week. I'll try to post one of these every week. If you want me to talk about anything, scream really loud because I havn't set up any communication between me and you yet. I'm probably going to fix this tomorrow and half this stuff will be gone so I don't know. Read or don't read I don't really care. This is just really therapeutic for me.
