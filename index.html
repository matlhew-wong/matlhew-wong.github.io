About

I found many of my current interests just by working with my professors. These include, number theory (prime factorization, RSA Encryption), machine learning, and relational databases.

But as much as a I love learning on the fly, it's important to continue improving yourself everyday which is why I try to always occupy some free time with learning something new so that when I need to use it, It's ready.
